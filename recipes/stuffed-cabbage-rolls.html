Stuffed Cabbage Rolls

A colorful entree for cold and dark autumn evenings.

You could replace ground beef with turkey for the lighter option but we are going to stick with the original Eastern European recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large head green cabbage
  • 1 lbs raw lean ground beef
  • 1/2 tbsp chopped garlic
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • salt and pepper

Directions:

  1. Bring cabbage to boil
  2. Drain cabbage and set aside to cool
  3. Mix beef, chopped garlic, salt and pepper
  4. Remove leaves from cabbage head
  5. Wrap leaves around beef mix to create rolls
  6. Place the rolls seam down in a pot and cover it with marinara sauce
  7. Set temperature to low and cook for 1 hour
