Potato Pancake

Delicious simplicity!

The aroma of crispy potato pancakes always brings me back to my childhood.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large Yukon gold potatoes
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • vegetable or canola oil

Directions:

  1. Grate the potatoes and onion with a food processor
  2. Squeeze out all the water into a bowl with mesh strainer
  3. Add egg, flour, 1 teaspoon salt, and pepper to potato/onion mesh and mix it
  4. Fry pancakes for 4-5 minutes on each side
